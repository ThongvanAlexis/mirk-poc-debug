- **NEW Q5 zoom-scramble FAILED — root cause diagnosed** via three chronological developer verbatim observations:
  1. Surface symptom: *"however zoom still scramble the mirk (rapid change of coordinate)"*.
  2. Off-anchor diagnostic: *"when zooming it at maximum power blue dot is on the 84 square, when zooming out at maximum blue dot is at 92 ... I think the issue is that the mirk 'center' isn't the blue dot of when we open the map, instead it must be a point outside of the screen and when zooming the shader is enlarged/shrinked relative to that point."*
  3. Cell-size definitive root cause: *"I tried zooming with the numbered shader, we can clearly see the number sliding (panning) fast, however I should see the number getting bigger, yet whatever the zoom level is, their size stay the same, so I don't know what is happening in fact."*
- **Mechanism diagnosed (Web-Mercator pixel-space anchor + raw-pixel cell size):**
  1. **Phenomenon 1 — Numbers slide fast during zoom:** Plan 03.1-10's FOG-17 formulation `worldPx = fragUv * uResolution + uPixelOrigin; noiseUv = worldPx / kNoiseTilePx` anchors noise to **Web-Mercator world-pixel space**. `uPixelOrigin` doubles per zoom level (Web-Mercator scales `256 * 2^zoom` px wide). Each zoom step doubles the world-pixel coordinate → the noise texture sampled at the blue dot shifts by `1.064M / 384 ≈ 2770` cells per zoom step.
  2. **Phenomenon 2 — Numbers stay the same size:** `kNoiseTilePx = 384.0` is a shader-side const in **raw-pixel units**. Cells are 384 raw px on screen at every zoom level (~128 logical px on iPhone 17 Pro at DPR=3). Cells pinned in screen-space, NOT to ground meters.
- **NEW v1 requirement FOG-18 added** — noise pattern anchored to ground meters, not pixel space. Plan 03.1-12 fix path: **Fix B world-meter anchor (DEVELOPER-ENDORSED).** Replace pixel-space anchor with meter-space anchor: `worldMeters = (fragUv * uResolution + uPixelOrigin) * uMetersPerPixel; noiseUv = worldMeters / kNoiseTilePxMeters`. Add uniform `uMetersPerPixel` (slot 41 → 42; under shader budget cap of 64). Dart-side: `metersPerPixel = 156543.03392 * cos(camera.center.latitude * π/180) / pow(2.0, camera.zoom)`. New const `kNoiseTilePxMeters = 1024.0` (1 km cell at hike scale). Result: noise position is zoom-invariant in meter-space; cells scale on screen with zoom naturally.
