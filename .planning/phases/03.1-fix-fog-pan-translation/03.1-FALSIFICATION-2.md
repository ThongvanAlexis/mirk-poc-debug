## Hypothesis

After the Plan 03.1-04 SHADER-MODULO-WRAP fix (uniform renamed `uOffset` → `uPixelOrigin`; modulo applied per-fragment via `fract(uPixelOrigin / uResolution)`) AND the Plan 03.1-05 CANVAS-FRAME-ALIGNMENT fix (`_FogPainter.paint()` reads `canvas.getTransform()` and pre-shifts the clip-path holes by `-(matrix[12], matrix[13])`), the same-Canvas fog now (a) translates with the camera during pan, (b) maintains a stable noise pattern across consecutive paints, and (c) keeps the reveal hole co-located with the blue dot during pan/zoom/combined gestures.

Re-stated against the original POC core value: the fog-of-war stays perfectly locked to the map during pan, zoom, and combined gestures on a sideloaded iOS build. **CONFIRMED-AFTER-FIX** means the Phase 3 hypothesis is reinstated and the POC ports back to MirkFall with all of Plan 03.1-02 + 03.1-04 + 03.1-05 fixes included; **DENIED-FINAL** means even the layered fix doesn't deliver fog-camera-tracking parity and the POC produces the formal architectural counter-evidence; **ITERATING** means a residual failure mode surfaced that has a clear next-iteration fix axis (Plan 03.1-07+).
